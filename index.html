Welcome

Hello, I'm Kazuhiro Takemoto (竹本和広), a professor at Kyushu Institute of Technology in Japan. My research focuses on developing novel computational methods in network science and AI with applications to cutting-edge challenges in biomedical, environmental, and social sciences.
